C. Description of the innovation

The OPSI’s working definition of public sector innovation is the implementation of a new way in which an organisation operates or the services that it provides. It should result in improvements in at least one of the following areas:
1) efficiency;
2) effectiveness;
3) service quality; or
4) user satisfaction
(Please note that you may still provide examples of innovations where the results are not yet available, with the opportunity to update these results at a later date).

E. Developing the Innovation

Design: Generating the idea for an innovation and turning into a project including his key attributes (budget, staff, tools)

Testing: The development and testing of an innovation e.g. by trialling, prototyping or piloting the innovation on a small scale.

Implementation: The rolling out of the innovation to reach its target beneficiaries.

Diffusion: Extending the innovation to other services or organisations; this may include the growing or scaling up of an innovation.
