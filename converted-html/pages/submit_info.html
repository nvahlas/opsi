Submitting an innovation

The Observatory of Public Sector Innovation collects experiences of developing public sector innovations from practitioners working in the public sector.

Submitting an innovation to the Observatory is simple:
1

Register		 2

Complete and submit online form		 3

OECD review *		 4

Preview		 5

Your innovation is published online

*National representatives to the OPSI project may also review a preview of your innovation.

If you currently work in the public administration in an OECD or key partner country and you would like to submit an example of an innovation to the Observatory please email opsi@oecd.org providing:

  • Your name
  • Your country
  • Your professional position
  • The name of the organisation where you work
  • The level of government of your organisation (i.e. central, state/regional or local government)
  • The name of the innovation that you would like to submit

If you do not work in a public administration where you country does not fit into one of the categories above, please contact the OPSI team at opsi@oecd.org.
